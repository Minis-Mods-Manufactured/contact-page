Frequently Asked Questions

What financing options are available?

We work with trusted lenders who specialize in manufactured home construction loans, including FHA, VA, USDA, and conventional options. Even if your credit isn’t perfect, we may still have a solution! Most of our buyers do NOT have perfect credit.

Can I tour a home before I apply?

Absolutely! In fact, I encourage it. Let's schedule a VIP tour to walk through homes, ask questions, and explore all the features in person.

How do I start the home buying process?

It all starts with touring our homes. Once we find your dream home, we'll complete an application and get you linked up with our lenders.

Can I place a home on my own land?

Yes! Many of our buyers place homes on private land. I can help walk you through the process.

Do you have land/home packages?

Yes, we do! While we don’t have homes already set up on land, we help you build a custom package that includes the home, land, and all necessary site work. We believe you should have the freedom to choose where you want to live—that’s why we don’t pre-place homes on land. Most customers pick their home first, then we help with financing and finding land that fits your needs and budget.

How much money do I need to get started?

It's free to apply! Once we get your approval back from the bank, the dealer will require a $1000 deposit to hold a home/order one. Down payment requirements will vary and depend on your credit and lender program. We’ll guide you through your options!

Will applying for financing hurt my credit?

Not the way you might think! When you’re shopping for a mortgage, credit bureaus expect you to compare lenders. All mortgage credit checks done within a 45-day window count as just one inquiry on your credit report.

The score impact is usually minimal — often just a few points — and the long-term savings from getting a better rate far outweigh that dip. Knowing your credit position early also helps us match you with the right lenders and programs from the start.

“I don’t want to pull my credit yet” is a common worry, but applying gives you real buying power and clarity — without locking you into anything.

What upgrades or customizations are available?

Options vary by builder and model. We’ll go over all available features during your visit.
